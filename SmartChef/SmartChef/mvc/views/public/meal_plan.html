Daily Plan Generation Rules

You can generate a meal plan only once per day.

If you have already generated a plan today, pressing the button will load your previously generated plan using the exact preferences that were used earlier today.

If you have not generated a plan today, the system will generate a new plan based on your current settings.

Tip: Review your preferences carefully before generating — they cannot be changed until tomorrow.
